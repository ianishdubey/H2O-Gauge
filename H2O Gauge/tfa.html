What Can Consumers Do?


Take a look at our footprint calculators to find out what in your daily life most affects your water footprint.
Making just a few changes can significantly reduce your water footprint.


  • Taking a bath with a bucket will lead to less usage of water as compared to a shower.
  • Using front load washing machine would result in less usage of water as compared to top-load or washing clothes with hands
  • Using a dishwasher consumes 5 times less water than washing it manually(with hands).
  • A regular car wash using a hose consumes 150 litres of water in a single use. Using a bucket consumes much less water
  • Using the half flush in the toilet can help reduce your water footprint by 4 litres for every time you flush


About

The water footprint is a measure of human beings' appropriation of fresh water in volumes of water consumed and/or polluted.We aim to create a society where freshwater is fairly shared among all human communities as well as nature’s diversity.We address the issue of water scarcity in India and promote proper utilization and less waste of water through technology and education.
